About Marjorie

You don't have to
navigate this alone

Are you feeling stuck in your relationship, overwhelmed by family dynamics, or seeking personal growth? I specialize in helping individuals and families navigate life's challenges using a compassionate, integrative approach.

Change, even when necessary, can feel overwhelming. Whether you're navigating divorce, healing from trauma, overcoming anxiety, or breaking free from codependent patterns — I provide a safe, affirming space for clarity, empowerment, and healing.

I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist and hold a Master of Social Work. With over 10 years of clinical practice, I draw from both evidence-based and mindfulness-informed modalities to meet each client exactly where they are.

My Philosophy

A tailored,
holistic approach

Therapy is not one-size-fits-all. I believe that treatment should be tailored to the unique needs and goals of each individual. My integrative approach blends classical evidence-based practices with eastern mindfulness-based techniques.

  • 01
    Humanistic & Bowenian

    I hold a humanistic, family-systems view — honoring the whole person within their relational context.

  • 02
    Evidence-Based Methods

    CBT, Emotionally Focused Therapy, Strength-Based, and Relational approaches form the clinical foundation.

  • 03
    Mindfulness & Presence

    Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT) and somatic awareness support deep, lasting healing.

  • 04
    Trauma-Informed Care

    All sessions are held in an affirming, trauma-informed space where safety and trust are paramount.
