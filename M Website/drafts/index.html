About

Marjorie Kennedy brings warmth, wisdom, and deep professional expertise to every client relationship. With years of experience working alongside individuals and families, she is committed to helping people find their footing through life's most meaningful transitions.

Her approach blends evidence-based methods with genuine human connection — creating a space that is both challenging and deeply supportive. Whether you are facing a crossroads, seeking growth, or simply searching for greater peace, Marjorie meets you exactly where you are.

My Philosophy

My Approach

Every person's journey is unique. I work alongside you — not ahead of you — listening carefully and tailoring each session to what you bring. You are the expert on your own life; my role is to help you access the wisdom already within you.
I draw from research-supported frameworks including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based approaches, and attachment theory — grounding our work in what the evidence shows is most effective for your goals.
Mental, emotional, and physical well-being are deeply interconnected. Our sessions attend to the full picture — your relationships, lifestyle, values, and sense of purpose — not just the presenting concern.
Creating a genuinely safe space is foundational to everything I do. You will always be met with warmth, honesty, and respect — and what you share stays here.
There is no fixed timeline for growth. We move at a pace that honors both your readiness and your resilience. Progress is celebrated at every stage — even the small, quiet shifts.
