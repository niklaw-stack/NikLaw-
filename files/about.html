About the Firm

IP Counsel Built on Decades of Experience

The Law Offices of Nikitas E. Nicolakis is a boutique intellectual property practice serving clients from emerging startups to established public corporations. The firm was founded on the principle that clients deserve sophisticated, high-caliber legal counsel delivered with personal attention and responsiveness.


With deep expertise in patent prosecution — both domestic and international — as well as infringement and validity opinions, the firm provides strategic IP advice that aligns with each client's unique business objectives. Our practice also encompasses trademark law, copyright, trade secrets, and related federal and state matters.


Prior to founding the firm, Nikitas E. Nicolakis honed his craft at prominent New York and national law firms, and served as a Supervising Assistant District Attorney in Rockland County, bringing a prosecutor's instinct for strategy and advocacy to every matter.
